# Contributing · Contribuição

🌐 Pick your language / Escolha o idioma:

Quick essentials (full details in the per-language guides):

- **Conventional Commits** — enforced locally by commitlint + husky and in CI.
- **Bilingual parity** — update `en-US/` and `pt-BR/` together and keep the 🌐 switcher.
- **Defensive scope only** — no payloads, functional malware, or operationalizable evasion.
- **Verified links** — access every reference before adding it; record date and version.

See also the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) and the [Security Policy](SECURITY.md).
