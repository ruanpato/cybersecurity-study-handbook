## Third-party material

- **Copyright-protected standards** (e.g., ISO/IEC 27001/27002) are **not** reproduced. The
  handbook only describes their **role** and points to the official sources (see
  [REFERENCES.md](REFERENCES.md) and [HANDBOOK Ch. 25](HANDBOOK.md#chapter-25)).
- **Open frameworks** (NIST CSF, MITRE ATT&CK/D3FEND, OWASP, CIS) have their own terms of use.
  Cite the source and respect each one's conditions when reusing their content.
- **Trademarks** (product, vendor, platform names) belong to their respective owners and are
  cited only for educational and reference purposes.

## Citation

When reusing excerpts from this material, cite the repository and version (see the
[HANDBOOK.md](HANDBOOK.md) cover). When citing external sources, use the information in
[REFERENCES.md](REFERENCES.md) and verify the links.

## Data and privacy

The exercises use **fictional data** and your own/authorized assets. Do not include personal
data, secrets, or real incident evidence in this repository (see [.gitignore](../.gitignore) and
[HANDBOOK Ch. 0](HANDBOOK.md#chapter-0)).
