# Progress Checklist

🌐 **Language:** **English (en-US)** · [Português (pt-BR)](../pt-BR/CHECKLIST-DE-PROGRESSO.md) · [README](README.md)

An instrument for **evidence-based self-assessment**. Use it monthly, alongside
[HANDBOOK.md](HANDBOOK.md) and [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md).

> **Golden rule:** mastery requires **evidence**, not a feeling. Only mark a level if you can
> point to the proof (documented lab, script in Git, write-up, report). "I think I got it" does
> not count.

---

## Mastery levels

For each item, classify your current level:

| Level | Label | Typical evidence |
|:----:|-------|------------------|
| 0 | Not started | — |
| 1 | Superficial knowledge | I read/watched, I know what it is |
| 2 | I can explain | I wrote a summary / explain it to someone |
| 3 | I can do it with guidance | I reproduced a lab following a guide |
| 4 | I can do it alone | I did it without a guide, from scratch |
| 5 | I can diagnose problems | I fixed it when something went wrong |
| 6 | I can teach | I wrote a write-up / taught someone |
| 7 | I can design solutions | I designed something new for a problem |
| 8 | I can produce original research | I generated new knowledge (CVE, technique, tool) |

Note the date and the evidence next to each mark. Suggested notation:
`[level] — YYYY-MM-DD — link/file of the evidence`.

## Periodic self-assessments

Each assessment must require **evidence**, not the subjective feeling of mastery.

### Monthly (30 min)

- Which levels went up? What evidence proves it?
- Which labs/projects did I complete?
- Where did I get stuck, and why?
- Concrete next steps for the following month.

### Quarterly (1–2 h)

- Am I following the current [ROADMAP](ROADMAP.md) phase? Do I need to adjust deadlines?
- Are fundamentals gaps reappearing? (If so, review before advancing.)
- Has my portfolio grown demonstrably?

### Semiannual (half a day)

- Reassess the stage (1–6) you are in (see [HANDBOOK](HANDBOOK.md#about-this-book)).
- Does the chosen (or candidate) specialization still make sense? (See
  [SPECIALIZATIONS.md](SPECIALIZATIONS.md).)
- What did I produce publicly (write-up, contribution, talk)?

### Annual (1 day)

- Full checklist review with evidence.
- Update the 12/24/36-month goals of the
  [ROADMAP](ROADMAP.md#suggested-plans-by-horizon).
- Identify 3 priority gaps and 3 milestones for the next year.
